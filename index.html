★ Your Party Destination

About Sea Cliff, NY

✦

One Square Mile. 250 Years of America. One Very Good Party.

Perched atop a 120-foot bluff overlooking Long Island Sound, just 25 miles east of Manhattan, Sea Cliff is one of Long Island's most charming villages. Known as the "Volunteer Village" — one square mile, 16 parks, its own beach, and some of the finest Victorian architecture in Nassau County.

On July 4th, the bay views are nothing short of spectacular. The fireworks over Long Island Sound are the stuff of legend. Or they will be, once Colin has finished telling everyone about them.

🏛️

Originally "Muskeeta Cove"

Originally inhabited by the Matinecock people. Purchased in the 1600s by Englishman Joseph Carpenter. The name means "cove of grassy flats."

⛪

Born as a Camp Meeting Ground

In the 1870s, Methodist families reached the clifftop by cable car for summer retreats. Their tents became cottages, then Victorian mansions.

📖

The Gold Coast

Sea Cliff sits on the Gold Coast immortalized in The Great Gatsby. Ours is better though.
